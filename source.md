# Philosophy
*Intellectual masturbation.*

Due to my limited vocabulary, I'll call it **Whateverism**:
A life constituting most, if not all, that existence has to offer: a beginning rooted in learning, a journey of seeking knowledge, and an end seasoned with wisdom.

In between: joy and depression, tragedy and loss, love and hate, mercy and acceptance. Some failure, a few rejections, more success, with equal reflection. 
To have experienced it all with open intentions; the heights and the hollows, the mid and the mild, the *some*, the *all*, the *none*, and the *void*.
